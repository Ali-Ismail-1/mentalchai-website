## Crispy Potatoes

### Tools

- Bowl to wash potatoes
- Potato Peeler
- Cutting Board
- Santoku Knife
- Baking Sheet

### Ingredients

- Yukon golden potatoes
- Salt
- Paprika
- Garam Masala
- Garlic powder
- Olive Oil

### Steps

```
- Cut potatoes into small cubes
- Season with salt, paprika, garam masala, galic, and olive oil
- Mix well
- Oven bake for 22min at 400F
```
